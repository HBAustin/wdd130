Why this Site?

This site is designed to be as simple as possible, while also attempting to be as compelling and user friendly as possible. It is, of course, impossible for everyone to be interested in my site, whether it’s due to design decisions or if you feel you are looking for something else. And that’s okay. If you decide not to use the site, I ask that you share the site with someone else that you think may be interested. If you do like the site and decide to hire me, more people are likely to be interested in my services. This is the most effective way for me to get more customers. A bad customer experience will push people away, so I will do everything in my power to keep any and all users happy
